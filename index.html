About Me

A creative and detail-oriented UI/UX Designer and Full-Stack Engineer with 2.5+ years of industry experience at hSenid Mobile Solutions, specializing in frontend engineering, modern UI design, WordPress development, and mobile app development. I have strong hands-on experience building scalable web dashboards, mobile applications, and custom digital solutions using React, Next.js, Flutter, and MERN stack.

My design philosophy centers around creating user-centered solutions that are both beautiful and functional. I believe in the power of clean, minimalistic design that enhances user experience without overwhelming the interface. Every project I work on is approached with attention to detail, performance optimization, and accessibility.

Beyond my full-time role, I actively freelance for SMEs, delivering high-quality landing pages, WordPress sites, and mobile apps. I'm passionate about staying current with the latest technologies and design trends, continuously learning and applying new skills to create better digital experiences.
